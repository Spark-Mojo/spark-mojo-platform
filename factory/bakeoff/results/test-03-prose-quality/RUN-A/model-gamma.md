**Last updated:** April 9, 2026
**For:** Therapists, front desk staff, billing coordinators, and office managers

---

## Introduction

WorkboardMojo is your daily task list inside Spark Mojo. It holds every piece of work that needs attention at your practice. Calling patients back. Chasing missing insurance forms. Approving intake schedules. You can see tasks, claim open ones, add notes, hand them off, and mark them done. It replaces sticky notes and scattered emails with one shared board.

---

## Who Uses This

- **Front desk staff** track scheduling, intake paperwork, and patient callbacks.
- **Billing coordinators** manage claims, denials, and prior authorizations.
- **Therapists** follow up on clinical requests and documentation gaps.
- **Office managers** monitor workload distribution and spot bottlenecks.

If you log into Spark Mojo, this board belongs to you.

## How To [Key Workflows]

### Open Your Board
1. Log in to Spark Mojo.
2. Find the window named "My Tasks" or "Workboard" on your desktop canvas.
3. Click it to open.

### Read Your Task List
You will see rows of tasks. A thin colored stripe on the left edge shows priority:
- **Red** = Urgent
- **Orange** = High
- **Teal** = Medium
- **Gray** = Low

Columns show the title, type, status, due date, and who owns it. Overdue tasks show a red "Overdue" label.

### Claim an Open Task
1. Find a row with a red "Unassigned" badge.
2. Click the "Claim" button on the right.
3. The task jumps to you. The status switches to In Progress.

### Finish a Task
1. Click the task row. A detail panel slides in from the right.
2. Scroll to the bottom.
3. Click the coral "Complete Task" button.
4. The panel closes. The task leaves your active list.

### Add a Note or Comment
1. Open the task.
2. Scroll to the Comments section.
3. Type your update in the text box.
4. Press Enter or click Send. It saves immediately.

### Pass a Task to a Coworker
1. Open the task.
2. Click the pencil icon next to "Details".
3. Type your coworker's name in the Assigned User field and select them.
4. Click Save.
5. Add a comment explaining why you handed it off.

### Change the Status
1. Open the task.
2. Use the Status dropdown near the top.
3. Pick New, Ready, In Progress, Waiting, Blocked, or Canceled.
4. If you pick Blocked or Failed, you must type a reason in the box that appears.
5. Click Confirm.

### Create a New Task
1. Click the "New Task" button in the toolbar.
2. Fill in a clear title.
3. Pick a task type (Action, Review, Approval).
4. Set the priority level.
5. Assign it to a person or a role.
6. Set a due date.
7. Click Create. It appears in the list right away.

### Switch Between Views
- Click **List** to see rows. Good for scanning due dates.
- Click **Kanban** to see columns by status. Good for visualizing workflow.
- Drag cards between columns in Kanban view to change status.
- Your last view is saved automatically.

### Find Finished Work
Click "Show Completed" in the filter bar. Old tasks appear with a faded look and a strikethrough title. Click "Show Completed" again to hide them.

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. **Where did my task go? I had it on my list and now it is gone.** Completed tasks hide from the main view. Click "Show Completed" to see finished work.

2. **Why does a task have a red pulsing badge?** It is assigned to a role, not a person. It needs someone to claim it.

3. **What does Overdue mean?** The due date has passed and the task is still open.

4. **Can I undo a completed task?** Not directly. Ask your office manager to change the status back in the admin system.

5. **How do I see who finished something?** Open the completed task. Expand the State History section. You will see the name and timestamp of the person who clicked Complete.

6. **Can two people work on one task at the same time?** Only one person owns it at a time. Use the Comments section to coordinate if you are collaborating.

7. **What if I click Claim at the exact same time as someone else?** You will see an error message saying the task was already claimed. Your coworker just beat you to it. Pick the next open task.

8. **Why is a task showing Blocked?** Someone hit a barrier. Open the task and read the status reason in the details to understand the hold-up.

9. **Can I create a task for someone else?** Yes. When making a new task, fill in their name in the Assigned User field. It lands on their board.

10. **Do I only see my own tasks?** WorkboardMojo shows every task for the entire practice. This keeps small teams aligned.

11. **Can I print my task list?** There is no print button yet. Use your browser's print shortcut (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P) as a workaround.

12. **Why do the top stat cards show different numbers than my list?** If a filter is active (like a Source dropdown or type tab), the list shrinks. The stat cards still count everything. Clear your filters to match the numbers.

13. **What is the difference between New and Ready?** New means it just arrived. Ready means prerequisites are met and it can start immediately.

14. **How do I delete a task?** You cannot delete tasks. Change the status to Canceled to remove it from active work. It stays in records.

15. **Can I set a reminder or alert for a task?** Alerts are not available yet. Check your overdue and due-today items each morning.

16. **What does Waiting mean?** The task is paused. You are waiting on a patient callback, an insurance response, or a coworker's input.

17. **How far back does completed history go?** Finished tasks are never deleted. You can search them anytime using "Show Completed".

18. **Can I reopen a finished task?** Ask your system admin to switch the status back to In Progress or Ready.

19. **I accidentally claimed the wrong task. Now what?** Open the task. Click the pencil icon. Clear your name from the Assigned User field or assign it to the correct person. Click Save.

20. **The board froze or shows an endless spinner.** Your internet connection likely dropped. Reload the page. If it persists, notify your office manager.

## Glossary

- **Role Queue:** A pool of tasks assigned to a job title (like "Billing" or "Front Desk") instead of a specific person. Anyone with that role can claim these tickets.
- **Unassigned / Unowned:** A task waiting in the role queue. It shows a red badge until someone takes it.
- **Kanban:** A column-based view. Tasks sit in buckets like New, Ready, or In Progress. You drag cards between columns to update status.
- **Drawer:** The side panel that slides in when you click a task. It holds all details, comments, and action buttons.
- **Canonical State / Status:** The official lifecycle stage of a task. Examples: New, In Progress, Blocked, Completed.
- **Source System:** Where the task originated. Manual means you created it. EHR or n8n means another system generated it automatically.
- **SLA:** Service Level Agreement. The expected number of hours to finish a task. Breach tracking is planned but not yet active.
